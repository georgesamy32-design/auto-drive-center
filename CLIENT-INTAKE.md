content/business info to collect from the client (send this to them). **Part 2**
is the technical accounts & assets needed to launch. Items are tagged:

- 🔴 **REQUIRED** before launch
- 🟡 **Recommended** (launch is possible without, but weaker)
- 🟢 **Phase 2** (add after launch)

## PART 1 — Questions for the Client

### 1. Business Basics & Identity 🔴
- [ ] Exact legal/trading name (Arabic + English) as it should appear.
- [ ] Short tagline / slogan (Arabic + English). Current: "Expert Car Care, Led by Engineers".
- [ ] One-paragraph "About us" in the client's own words (Arabic + English).
- [ ] Year the business was established (drives the "Years of Expertise" stat).
- [ ] Commercial registration / tax ID number (if it must appear in the footer).

### 2. Contact & Location 🔴
- [ ] Confirmed single address (Arabic + English) — exact street, landmark, area.
- [ ] Google Maps pin/link for that address (or a "what3words"/landmark so we can place it).
- [ ] All phone numbers + what each is for (sales, workshop, emergency, complaints).
- [ ] WhatsApp number (can be one of the above) — see §11.
- [ ] Public email address (current: auto.drive2024@outlook.com — keep or use a domain email?).
- [ ] Is there more than one branch after all? If yes, full details for each.

### 3. Working Hours 🔴
- [ ] Opening hours per day (including Friday / public holidays).
- [ ] Any "emergency / 24h" service line? (affects the hero & top bar).
- [ ] Ramadan / seasonal hours? (optional note).

### 4. Branding & Visual Identity 🔴
- [ ] Logo files — vector preferred (`.svg`, `.ai`, `.pdf`) or high-res PNG. (Currently we show an "ADC" text mark.)
- [ ] Brand colors — keep the current deep charcoal + red, or specific brand hex codes?
- [ ] Preferred fonts (if the brand has any) — otherwise we keep Sora/Cairo.
- [ ] Do they have brand guidelines / a profile/company deck we can pull from?

### 5. Services — detailed 🔴
For **each** service, collect:
- [ ] Service name (Arabic + English).
- [ ] 1–2 sentence description.
- [ ] Price or price range — or "starting from" — or "call for quote"? (Do they want prices shown publicly at all?)
- [ ] Estimated time / turnaround.
- [ ] Any service that should be the "headline" / most promoted?

Current 4 services to confirm/expand: Mechanical Repairs · Transmission Service (Shell) · Electrical & Tuning · Full Vehicle Renewal. Also confirm: diagnostics, oil change, AC, tires, body/paint, recovery/towing?
- [ ] Car brands they specialise in or refuse (e.g. European only? all brands?).
- [ ] Do they sell parts/fluids, or service only?
- [ ] Warranty offered on repairs? (big trust factor — duration/terms).

### 6. The Team / About the Engineer 🟡
- [ ] Eng. Mina Matta — correct title, full name spelling (Arabic + English), credentials/qualifications to highlight.
- [ ] Professional photo of him (and/or the team).
- [ ] Short bio / philosophy quote we can feature.
- [ ] Team size / key staff to mention?

### 7. Trust, Credentials & Proof 🟡
- [ ] Certifications, licenses, association memberships (logos if available).
- [ ] Official partner/authorized-dealer status (e.g. Shell) — can we display the logo? Any usage permission needed?
- [ ] Equipment/brands they want to showcase (diagnostic tools, etc.).
- [ ] Real numbers for the stats: years in business, cars/customers served, etc.

### 8. Offers & Promotions 🟡
- [ ] Any current promotions/discounts to feature? (e.g. "Free diagnostic with any service", "10% off first visit").
- [ ] Seasonal campaigns (summer AC check, winter prep, back-to-school)?
- [ ] Loyalty / referral program? (points, repeat-visit discount, "refer a friend").
- [ ] Service packages/bundles (e.g. "Full Maintenance Package")? List + pricing.
- [ ] Gift vouchers?
- [ ] How often will offers change? (decides whether we build an editable "Offers" section/banner.)

### 9. Insurance & Partnerships 🟢→🟡
- [ ] Do they work with insurance companies for accident/repair claims? Which ones?
- [ ] Are they an approved repair center for any insurer? (display logos + "we handle the paperwork" messaging).
- [ ] Process for insurance customers (what should the page tell a claimant to do/bring?).
- [ ] Bank/financing or "pay in installments" partnerships (e.g. valU, Souhoola, ContactCars)?
- [ ] Roadside assistance / recovery partnerships?

### 10. Corporate / Fleet / B2B Clients 🟢→🟡
- [ ] Do they service company fleets / corporate accounts? Want to actively pursue them?
- [ ] Existing notable corporate clients (logos/names we can show — with permission)?
- [ ] Fleet offering details: contract maintenance, priority service, volume pricing, pickup & delivery, monthly invoicing?
- [ ] A dedicated "For Businesses / Fleet" page or section + a separate B2B contact (person, phone, email)?
- [ ] Minimum fleet size / terms?
- [ ] Any B2B brochure / rate card to upload?

### 11. Booking & Lead Handling 🔴
- [ ] Where should booking-form submissions go? (which email inbox, and/or WhatsApp, and/or a phone to follow up).
- [ ] Who receives & responds to leads, and how fast (sets expectations on the site).
- [ ] Should the form also send the customer an auto-confirmation (email/WhatsApp)?
- [ ] Fields to keep/add/remove: Name, Phone, Car Model, Date, Service, Notes — anything else (license plate, mileage, preferred branch/time)?
- [ ] Do they want real-time online scheduling (pick a slot) or just a request that they confirm by phone?
- [ ] WhatsApp Business: which number, and a default pre-filled message ("Hi, I'd like to book…")?

### 12. Photos & Media 🔴
- [ ] Real photos: workshop exterior & interior, equipment, team at work, before/after repairs, the reception/waiting area.
- [ ] A hero image/video for the top of the page (the single biggest visual upgrade).
- [ ] Logo + favicon (small icon for browser tabs).
- [ ] Any existing video (workshop tour, intro)?
- [ ] Permission/rights confirmed for all images (especially customer cars/insurer logos).

### 13. Testimonials & Reviews 🟡
- [ ] 3–6 customer testimonials (name, optional photo, car, quote) — with consent.
- [ ] Google Business Profile reviews link (to display/embed real reviews + star rating).
- [ ] Any press mentions / awards?

### 14. Social Media & Messaging 🟡
- [ ] Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn URLs (whichever exist).
- [ ] Should the site show a Facebook/Instagram feed, or just icons/links?
- [ ] Primary channel customers use to reach them (so we make it prominent).

### 15. Content, Tone & Language 🔴
- [ ] Primary language: Arabic, English, or both equally? (site is bilingual now).
- [ ] Tone: formal/corporate vs friendly/approachable?
- [ ] Who writes/approves the final Arabic copy (we have draft Arabic — needs a native review, esp. street names & service terms)?

### 16. SEO & Local Discovery 🟡
- [ ] Google Business Profile — does it exist & is it verified? (critical for "car mechanic near me" searches). We may need owner access to claim/optimize it.
- [ ] Target search areas/keywords (e.g. "ميكانيكي السلام", "صيانة فتيس El-Salam").
- [ ] Page title & meta description preferences (or we draft them).

### 17. Legal & Policies 🟡
- [ ] Privacy policy (required since the form collects personal data) — provide their own or we draft a standard one.
- [ ] Terms of service / service guarantee text.
- [ ] Cookie/consent notice needed? (depends on analytics).

### 18. Future / Phase 2 🟢
- [ ] Online payment / deposits to book?
- [ ] Customer portal (service history per car)?
- [ ] Live chat?
- [ ] Blog / maintenance tips (good for SEO)?
- [ ] Multi-branch support if they expand?
- [ ] Loyalty app / SMS campaigns?

## PART 2 — Technical & Accounts Needed to Launch

### Domain & Hosting 🔴
- [ ] **Domain name** chosen & purchased (e.g. `autodrivecenter.com` / `.net` / `.com.eg`). Who owns the registrar account?
- [ ] **Hostinger account** access (or we provision under your agency account and hand off).
- [ ] SSL certificate enabled (Hostinger provides free SSL — must be on for `https`).

### Email 🔴
- [ ] A professional domain email (e.g. `info@autodrivecenter.com`) or keep the Outlook address.
- [ ] SMTP details so the website's booking form can reliably send email (Hostinger email or an SMTP plugin/service).

### WhatsApp & Messaging 🔴
- [ ] WhatsApp Business number confirmed for the click-to-chat button.

### Maps & Local 🟡
- [ ] Exact Google Maps embed for the location.
- [ ] Google Business Profile owner access (to verify/optimize).

### Analytics & Tracking 🟡
- [ ] Google Analytics (GA4) account — to measure visits & form submissions.
- [ ] Meta (Facebook) Pixel — if they'll run Facebook/Instagram ads.

### Build Platform (your side) 🔴
- [ ] WordPress installed on Hostinger + Elementor (per `ELEMENTOR-BUILD-GUIDE.md`).
- [ ] Decision: ship the production site on WordPress/Elementor; keep this GitHub Pages link as the client-approval preview.

### Brand Assets 🔴
- [ ] Final logo (svg/png) + favicon.
- [ ] Real photography (see §12).

### Pre-launch QA checklist 🔴
- [ ] All phone numbers dial correctly (mobile test).
- [ ] WhatsApp button opens chat with pre-filled message.
- [ ] Booking form delivers to the right inbox + auto-reply works.
- [ ] Map opens the correct pin.
- [ ] Arabic page reviewed by a native speaker.
- [ ] Mobile, tablet, desktop tested.
- [ ] Page title, favicon, meta description, social-share preview set.
- [ ] Privacy policy linked in footer.
- [ ] 404 page + redirect from `www`/non-`www` working.

2. Confirmed single address + map pin
3. Working hours
4. Phone(s) + WhatsApp number
5. Final logo
6. At least a few real photos (hero + workshop)
7. Service list (with or without prices)
8. Booking-form destination email
9. Domain + Hostinger + SSL
10. Approved Arabic copy
